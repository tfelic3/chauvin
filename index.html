Sentencing of Derek Chauvin

On April 20, former Minneapolis police officer Derek Chauvin was found guilty on all charges in the death of George Floyd. It took the jury in Chauvin's case about 10 hours over a two-day period to return a guilty verdict for the white police officer. Chauvin is scheduled to be sentenced on June 25.
